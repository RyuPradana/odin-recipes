Homemade Fried Rice ingredients:

  • Cooked rice: Any kind of white or brown rice will do. Just be sure that it is cooked
  • Eggs:Which add great flavor, texture and protein.
  • Carrots, onions, green onions and peas: These are veggies that I used
  • Garlic:Minced
  • Soy Sauce:Great flavor
  • Oyster sauce:Great flavor too
  • Toasted Sesame Oil:Finishing touch
  • Butter:Add creaminess
